# Threshold declaration completeness — rule 0.1j (WO-016 §4.2)

**Date:** 2026-07-21 · **Context:** WO-008b-B-RERUN pong-state classification · **Author:** project lead

> "The lag threshold declared both a per-sample bound and a run-level fraction; the pong
> threshold declared only the bound. The run-level pong classification (27.1% late → LATE)
> therefore rested on an aggregation that may not have been pre-declared. The verdict stood
> on a 5× margin, but a closer result would have required exactly the after-the-fact
> interpretation pre-declaration exists to prevent. Standing consequence: a threshold is not
> fully declared until both its per-sample bound and its run-level aggregation are stated —
> bound, aggregation window, verdict fraction, all three, or the declaration is incomplete
> by form."

**Ratified as standing rule 0.1j.** A threshold declaration missing any of {per-sample bound,
aggregation window, verdict fraction} is incomplete by form.
