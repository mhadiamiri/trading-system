2. ApprovedOrder.price=0 — how did you resolve it, and why?
   ---------------------------------------------------------

   RESOLUTION: Kept the field with updated documentation.

   The price field represents ORDER INTENT (e.g., limit price for future limit orders), not fill price.
   For market orders, price=0 is a valid sentinel meaning "no price constraint." The venue determines the
   actual fill price from MarketState (observed bid/ask).

   UPDATED DOCUMENTATION:
   - src/trading/risk/engine.py: "Market order: venue determines fill price from MarketState"
   - src/trading/execution/approved_order.py: "price represents ORDER INTENT (not fill price)"
   - src/trading/execution/approved_order.py: "For market orders, price=0 (no constraint, venue determines fill price)"

   WHY: Removing the field would be a breaking change, and it may be needed for limit orders (future).
   The honest resolution is to document its purpose as order intent, not fill price.
